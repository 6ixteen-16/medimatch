About MediMatch

Uganda's community blood coordination and emergency response platform.

Our Mission

MediMatch bridges the gap between blood donors, community health centres, and regional blood banks across Uganda. Through real-time SOS coordination, automated donor matching, and transport support, we help ensure that no patient loses their life because blood couldn't reach them in time.

Built for Uganda

Designed with Uganda's healthcare context in mind — SMS notifications via Africa's Talking, district-level donor search, and a system that works even on slow connections.

Built at Makerere University, Kampala · 2026
